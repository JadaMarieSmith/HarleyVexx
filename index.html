About the Artist

HarleyVexx is a completely self-taught independent music artist from Salisbury, NC, known for blending poetic storytelling with rhythmic expression. Since going public in May 2025, HarleyVexx has gained over 625,000 streams across international platforms.

With a unique style that incorporates spoken word, R&B structures, and symphonic ear candy, HarleyVexx creates music that transforms emotions into powerful lyrical experiences. Every track is 100% independently owned, from rights to royalties to distribution.
